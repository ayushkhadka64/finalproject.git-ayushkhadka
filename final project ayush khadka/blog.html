E-Commerce

E-Commerce or electronic commerce is any type of commercial transactions over an electronic network, mainly through internet. e-Commerce started way back in 1960s when organizations began to use Electronic Data Interchange(EDI) to transfer documents of their business back and forth.

Online shopping is the best example of the e-commerce in present context.

There are three main categories of e-commerce.

  • B2B Business to Business
  • B2C Business to Consumer
  • C2C Consumer to Consumer

Advantages of E-Commerce

E-Commerce has adversely affected business in many ways. Some of the advantages of it in our business are as follow:

  • It is easy to start and manage online business.
  • E-Commerce allows to find the product faster along with fast buying and selling procedures with just one click.
  • There is much low cost to manage e-commerce website than to manage shop. Hence, its low operational cost and better quality of services makes it more effective.
  • It doesn't have any geographial limitations, i.e. customers can order from any part of the world.
